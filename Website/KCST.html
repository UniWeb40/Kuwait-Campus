Kuwait College of Science and Technology is a private university in Kuwait. KCST is located in Doha District, west of Kuwait City. KCST started its degree programs in 2016. Its operations are regulated by the Private Universities Council of Kuwait.

Degree Programs KCST offers

The structure and contents of KCST's initial programs, developed by the Indian Institute of Technology, Delhi (IITD), are carefully designed based on a synthesis of a variety of high-quality programs in computer science, computer engineering, electronics, and communication engineering. The academic consultancy provided by IITD augments the scientific strength of the University and ensures that future developments follow education standards comparable with IITD. The curricular are of international standard, designed to conform to the philosophy, recommendations and detailed contents endorsed by ACM and IEEE-CS, as well as the ABET accreditation requirements for engineering programs
